TESTIMONIAL

Eliud Kipchoge

World marathon runner Eliud Kipchoge wore Nike Air Zoom AlphaFly shoes when breaking the world marathon record. He stated that the shoes provided exceptional comfort and responsiveness, helping to enhance his performance while running.

Michael Jordan

As one of the most famous athletes to collaborate with Nike, Michael Jordan revealed that his partnership with Nike changed the way people view sports shoes. He mentioned that his hard work and dedication in training, along with Nike’s innovations, such as the launch of Nike Air Jordan, have inspired many people beyond the basketball court. 

Jacquemus

The collaboration between Nike and fashion designer Jacquemus brings a collection that blends fashion elements with sports technology. Although not widely known, this collection has gained attention for its unique and innovative design, attracting consumers looking for both style and performance.

Kelvin Kiptum

Renowned sprint athlete Allyson Felix shared her positive experience with Nike shoes. She said that Nike pays special attention to comfort and performance, making her feel more confident during races. Felix also emphasized that her collaboration with Nike has helped create products that suit both her athletic and lifestyle needs.
